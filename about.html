About Renara

Passionate about empowering women's rights organizations

Our Story: Built by Students, for Change

[EDIT: Add your founding story. Example: "In 2027, three high school students noticed that women's rights nonprofits were powerful but underfunded and under-resourced when it came to digital tools. What started as a class project became a mission."]

As young people growing up in a digital world, we understand what works online. We're not jaded by "the way things have always been done," and we bring genuine passion for women's rights to every project. Our student-led team has helped over 50 organizations transform their online presence and increase their impact.

We don't just build websites—we partner with organizations to develop comprehensive digital strategies that drive real change. And we do it at prices that work for nonprofits.

Proof that age isn't a barrier to impact—passion and execution are what matters.
