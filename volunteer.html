Volunteer & Get Involved

Join Renara in building a more inclusive, tech-enabled future

Be part of the mission

Renara is powered by students, volunteers, and community partners who care about making technology more welcoming, accessible, and empowering.

Whether you want to mentor students, help redesign websites for nonprofits, support outreach efforts, or share your skills in a new way, there are plenty of ways to get involved.

We believe that meaningful change grows through collaboration, and every person who joins our network helps us reach more people and create more opportunities for learning and impact.

Ways to help

  • • Mentor students interested in coding or design
  • • Support nonprofit website redesign projects
  • • Help with community outreach and partnerships
  • • Share your expertise in design, communications, or technology

For Students

Learn, collaborate, and build real-world digital experiences that make a difference.

For Volunteers

Bring your skills into a supportive, mission-driven community focused on empowerment and impact.

For Partners

Work with us to expand access, build community outreach, and create lasting opportunities for growth.
